TypeJump — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

TypeJump is a macOS menu bar utility that opens files, folders, and applications via global keyboard shortcuts. It is built to work entirely on your Mac.

Data Collection

TypeJump does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no network communication of any kind.

Local Data

Your shortcut configuration (names, file paths, and keyboard combinations) is stored only on your device. It never leaves your Mac and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

TypeJump does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect data.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this address with a new effective date.
